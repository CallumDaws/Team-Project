Campus Chase?

What?

Campus chase is a Treasure hunt application which challenges you to find different locations around the Newcastle University using the different clues given to you to help you find the objectives

Why?

During early discussions in our group we all agreed upon the fact that we all wanted to create a Treasure Hunt application for users to play as it is a fun game for users to play while also learning more about their campus

Who?

Our application was initially aimed at first year Newcastle University students as it has a direct impact on learning areas in the campus through fun interactive clue finding adventures but can be played by anyone in the area

How?

Our application is developed by our group using Android studio as our application is initially designed to be used on Android devices, we also have used MySQL for our database system

Where?

As we are Newcastle University students our application is currently based in the surrounding area, finding locations that are based around the Newcastle University campus

About Us

We are a group of second year university students which have been tasked to build a mobile application of our choice. As a group we quickly decided on the idea of Campus Chase after having researched about different location-finding applications, deciding that this idea would be a great idea to make as it not only is a fun game about finding different areas and collecting score to compete against others. Campus Chase also gives the users the ability to learn more about the Newcastle University campus as a whole.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the application work?

The application uses a map based system which will track your location to tell you where you currently are, you can then scan QR codes once you have found your location to recieve points, each clue you use will reduce the amount of points you will get for that location

Can i play on my own?

You can! Campus Chase allows you to play on your own if you wish to as you can create your own team and aren't required to invite any additional team members

What devices can i play this on?

Currently Campus Chase is available to be played on Android devices running "Android something" or higher

is Campus Chase free?

Campus Chase is available to be downloaded and played free of charge and there are no ingame transactions!

Can this be played offline?

Campus Chase requires a constant internet connection as it requires you to connect to the database to update your scores and to be able to create or join a team

How does the game track my score?

The game uses a database which updates your score every time you find a new location, each time you ask the game for a additional clue you lose a point for that location. You begin with 3 points per location and lose 1 point for each clue.

How do i join a friends team?

Once your friend has created their team they will be given a code which you are able to input on the join team screen which will add you to your friends team.

I put the name in wrong, can i change it?

Yes! Campus Chase allows you to change your name in-game even after you have created your profile by going into the settings menu.
